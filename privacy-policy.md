# Deejiar Privacy Policy  

**Effective Date: September 9, 2025**  

---

## 1. Data Collection  

We collect extensive data to enhance our services and business operations:  

### Behavioral Analytics  
- Complete browsing history, click patterns, scroll depth, session duration  
- Search queries, interaction patterns, content preferences  
- Device fingerprinting and cross-device tracking  
- Location history and movement patterns  
- Social interactions and connection graphs  

### Technical Data  
- IP addresses, device IDs, browser information  
- App usage metrics, feature engagement rates  
- Third-party app integrations and external activity  

---

## 2. Commercial Use of Data  

### User-Generated Content  
- All uploaded photos (except profile avatars) become part of Deejiar's commercial asset library  
- Content may be packaged, licensed, or sold to third parties  
- We may create derivative products or datasets from your content  

### Behavioral Data Monetization  
- We analyze and package user behavior patterns for commercial insights  
- Aggregated and anonymized data may be sold to:  
  - Market research firms  
  - Advertising networks  
  - Business intelligence platforms  
  - Academic institutions  

- **For non-EU users:** Pseudonymized individual-level data may be shared with partners where legally permissible  
- **For EU users:** Only aggregated, anonymized data will be commercialized unless explicit consent is provided  

---

## 3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR Compliance)  

For EU/EEA users, we process your data based on:  
- **Contract:** To provide our services  
- **Legitimate Interests:** For analytics, fraud prevention, and service improvement  
- **Consent:** For marketing and data commercialization beyond aggregated insights  
- **Legal Obligation:** When required by law  

---

## 4. Third-Party Sharing  

We share your information with:  
- **Business Partners:** For joint offerings and enhanced services  
- **Data Processors:** Analytics, advertising, and marketing platforms  
- **Commercial Buyers:** Organizations purchasing aggregated insights or licensed content  
- **Legal Compliance:** When required by law or subpoena  

### Opt-Out Limitations  
- **US Users:** Core data sharing necessary for service operation cannot be opted out of while maintaining an account  
- **EU Users:** You may opt out of non-essential data sharing while maintaining basic service access  

---

## 5. Data Retention  

- **Active account data:** Retained indefinitely  
- **Deleted account data:**  
  - **EU users:** Personal data deleted after 90 days, except where legitimate business or legal reasons require retention  
  - **Non-EU users:** Commercial Content and derived insights retained permanently  

- **Behavioral analytics:** Retained for 7 years minimum  

---

## 6. International Transfers  

Your data may be transferred to countries outside your residence, including the United States.  

- **EU Users:** Transfers are conducted under Standard Contractual Clauses or adequacy decisions  
- By using Deejiar, you consent to these transfers  

---

## 7. Your Rights  

### For All Users  
- **Access:** Request what data we hold (may include processing fee where permitted)  
- **Correction:** Update inaccurate information  
- **Deletion:** Request deletion (excludes Commercial Content already licensed, subject to legal retention requirements)  

### Additional Rights for EU/EEA Users  
- **Portability:** Receive your personal data in a structured, machine-readable format  
- **Restriction:** Request restriction of processing in certain circumstances  
- **Objection:** Object to processing based on legitimate interests  
- **Automated Decision-Making:** Right not to be subject to solely automated decisions  
- **Withdraw Consent:** Where processing is based on consent  

### Additional Rights for California Users  
- **Know:** Right to know what personal information is collected, used, shared or sold  
- **Delete:** Right to delete personal information (with exceptions)  
- **Opt-Out:** Right to opt-out of sale of personal information  
- **Non-Discrimination:** Right not to be discriminated against for exercising privacy rights  

> **Note:** Exercising these rights may result in limited functionality or account termination if it conflicts with service provision.  

---

## 8. Cookie Policy  

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies:  
- **Essential Cookies:** Required for service functionality  
- **Analytics Cookies:** To understand usage patterns  
- **Marketing Cookies:** For targeted advertising  

**EU users** will be prompted for consent for non-essential cookies.  

---

## 9. Children's Privacy  

- Deejiar is not intended for users under 18 (or 16 in certain EU countries)  
- We do not knowingly collect data from children  
- Content featuring minors may be included if uploaded by authorized account holders  

---

## 10. Data Security  

We implement industry-standard security measures including:  
- Encryption in transit and at rest  
- Regular security audits  
- Access controls and authentication  

However, no system is 100% secure.  

---

## 11. Breach Notification  

- **EU Users:** Notified within 72 hours if breach poses risk to rights and freedoms  
- **Other Users:** Notified as required by applicable law

- EU users may be required to re-consent for significant changes  
- Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance
